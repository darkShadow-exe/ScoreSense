## **Production Notes:**

### **Screen Recording Flow:**
1. **Browser Setup:** Chrome/Firefox with ScoreSense at localhost:5000
2. **Recording Software:** OBS/Camtasia with high-quality screen capture
3. **Audio:** Clear voiceover recorded separately, synced in post
4. **Cursor:** Highlight cursor movements for emphasis
5. **Smooth Transitions:** Quick fade/cut between sections

### **Key Screenshots/Actions:**
- **Dashboard Overview:** Hover over statistics, show data richness
- **Command Typing:** Slow, deliberate typing for readability  
- **Results Display:** Pause on success messages and results
- **Voice Demo:** Show microphone icon activation and "Listening..." state
- **Graph Loading:** Show smooth graph generation animations
- **Navigation:** Quick but clear movement between tabs

### **Demo Commands (Exact Text):**
1. `"Add student Emily Chen in grade 10 section A age 15"`
2. `"Add exam for Emily: Midterm with math 92, physics 88, chemistry 85, biology 90"`
3. `"Who is the class topper?"`
4. `"Who scored highest in mathematics?"`
5. `"Predict Emily's next math score"`
6. **Voice Command:** `"Show me class performance in physics"`

### **Audio Quality:**
- **Clear narration** with professional microphone
- **Consistent pacing** - not too fast, not too slow
- **Enthusiasm** without being overly energetic
- **Background music** (optional, subtle)
- **No echo or background noise**

### **Visual Emphasis:**
- **Highlight important UI elements** with subtle effects
- **Zoom in** on command input and results
- **Clean browser** - no distracting tabs or bookmarks
- **Fast loading** - pre-populate database for smooth demo
- **Smooth scrolling** when showing long lists

### **File Output:**
- **1080p HD** minimum resolution
- **MP4 format** for compatibility
- **3-4 minutes** total duration
- **Optimized for** YouTube, social media, presentations
